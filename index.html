About Me

Hi! My name is Kyle and I am a student at the University of North Florida majoring in computer science. Programming is not only what i pursue but what I am passionate about. It is like a hobby that's a job.

"Choose a job you love, and you will never have to work a day in your life."
-Confucius

My school teaches the core concepts of programming, as well as the ethics behind it. I would like to call myself a Java developer mostly due to the massive amounts of side projects that I do. However, web development is growing on me and I really enjoyed putting my website together. I am aspiring to be a full stack developer. Whether it be for a small start-up, or a large corporation, I would be stoked to be part of something bigger than myself.

I am going to start a blog in the future and write blogs about the curiculum that I am learning in school. This will serve as an educational blog to anyone that has interest in the field or needs additional knowledge on a topic.
